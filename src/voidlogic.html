About

VOIDLOGIC is the new alias of Ionut Popovici, formerly known for his trance productions under John Spider.

Now venturing into the realm of drum and bass, VOIDLOGIC brings a fresh, energetic approach to the genre, blending uplifting melodies with dancefloor-driven beats. His debut track, "Until You See the Sun," is an electrifying fusion of fast-paced rhythms and euphoric vibes, guaranteed to set the stage for his presence in the drum and bass scene.
